MNREGA

The Government of India passed the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 in September, 2005. The Act gives legal guarantee of a hundred days of wage employment in a financial year to adult members of a rural household who demand employment and are willing to do unskilled manual work

PM Vidhya Lakshmi Scheme

IT is designed to provide financial assistance to meritorious students for higher education. Aims to provide collateral and gauranteer free loan to students.Students with an annual income upto 3 lakhs can get 3% interest subsidy on loans upto 3 lakhs.

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

It aims to generate awareness and efficiency of wellfare services intended for girls. Provides paarents with income tax rebate benefits and an interest rate of 7.6% on deposit and the option to open a savings acc per girl child under the age of 10 yrs and provide Tax Free interest rate of 8.2% p.a

Swanath Scholarship Scheme

Provides scholarship scheme for children from certain categories like orphan, either parents died due to COVID, armed forces and Para military forces(martyr). Provides financial support of 50k p.a for study.

National Pension System

Is a retirement saving plan that allow indians to generate pension acc income after retirement. It offers tax incentives under income tax act of 1961. Citizens aged btwn 18 to 60 can fill out an online form and recieve permanent retiremnt acc number which is applicable for this scheme.

Pradhan MAntri Jeevan Jyoti Yojna

Is a 1yr term life insurance scheme taht offers a life insurance cover of 2 lakh in the event of death. Applicants must be aged btwn 18 to 50 yrs and have an indiavidual bak or a post office acc. The annual premium is 436 Rs which is automatically debited from te acc. CAn be applied via any Net Banking for PM.J.J.Y

Atal Pension Yojna

It is open to Indian citizen btwn age 18 to 40 who have an Aadhar card linked bank acc. It provides a gaurateed monthly penion of Rs 1k-5k after the age of 60. Subscriber pays a monthly contribution for the scheme debited from the acc. Pension continues to be paid to be spouse after death.

PM Suraksha Bima Yojna

An accidental insurance scheme offering insurance for accidental death and disability cover for death on account of accident. Premium of Rs 20 p.a per member for coverage under PMSBY. Is valid for 1 yr eligible for 18-70 yrs. In case a member is covered through more than 1 acc and premium is recieved by insurance comp, insurance would be restricted to 2 lakh
